Ajmat ji ki Dard bhari shayari

            Ro raha hu ek muddat se 
            Ho gya Ishq badi shiddat se 
            Tajarba hai is lie bata raha hu 
            Moat behtar hai mohabbat se.
            
            Tu pass reh ke v dur thi 
            Tu dur reh ke v dur hai 
            Tujh se piyar karna na chahu fir 
            ye dil majbur hai 
            Sab kehte hai ab wo teri nhi rahi 
            move on kar ja 
            Par ek tarfa piyar karne ka bhi 
            koi guroor hai 
            
                Kalam se likh na saka udas dil ke 
                afsane Mai use dil se chahta hu 
                Uski dil ki khuda jane
            
            Aeisi bhi kya khata kardi ham,
            ne jo qabil e maafi nhi 
            Aap ko dekha nhi kai dino se,
            kiya yahi saza kaafi nhi
            
            Apno ke saare lafz
            feeke par jaate hain,

            Jab khauf ke badal
            unke sar par chaate hain,

            Apno ko matlab tabhi tak tha...
            jab tak iss jism mein...
            rooh ka alfaaz jaari tha.
            
            Yu na tum udaas raho mere diwane, 
            Kya haal h tumhare Dil ka ye bas 
            Mera hi Dil jane
            
            Dua Krna dum v usi din nikle ,
            jis din tere dil se hum nikle
            
            Pran priye sangi saath hu tumhare path, 
            ke Andheri me mere jivan ke ujiyare
